ABOUT US


Great designers, like great musicians, never stop developing their skills and searching for new and unique solutions to the challenges they face. Comprised entirely of University of Waterloo Students, Fifth Bloc Designs takes great pride in our ability to help our customers achieve their goals by working to achieve our own. We go to great lengths to ensure that we are knowledgeable about our customers needs and wants, as well as fostering a meaningful relationship that continues long after projects are completed. This is extremely important to Fifth Bloc Designs. It allows us to regularly monitor our product’s performance, our customers satisfaction, as well as build a network of customers that connect with each other, do business, and benefit mutually. We also are very proud of our commitment to constantly educate ourselves and ensure that we possess the most current tools and knowledge required to make our customers dreams come true.
